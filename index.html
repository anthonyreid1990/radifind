Key Features

Automated Image Sharing

Easily send and receive radiology (DICOM) images between institutions with just a few clicks

Robust Security & Compliance

With end-to-end encryption and two-factor authentication (2FA), your data is safe. Compliant with HIPAA, AHPRA, and the Privacy Act 1988.

Seamless PACS & RIS Integration

RadiFind integrates with existing systems to pull images on demand, ensuring fast, accurate retrieval.

User-Friendly Dashboard

Quickly see who wants to access your patient's images and control user/patient visibility

Protecting patient data through robust security and privacy protocols

Our platform integrates advanced security features and strict privacy policies to ensure sensitive information is handled with the highest level of care. From encryption and authentication to audit logs and data access control, RadiFind has you covered.

Built for Compliance

RadiFind complies with industry standards like HIPAA, AHPRA, and the Privacy Act 1988, ensuring responsible management of patient data and upholding security and privacy across Australasia.

End-to-End Encryption

All data, including DICOM images, is encrypted from transmission to storage, ensuring that only authorized individuals can access patient information.

Two-Factor Authentication

Every account is secured with 2FA, adding an extra layer of verification to keep unauthorized users out.

Data Access Control & Permissions

Permissions are configured by clinic admins, allowing only authorized personnel to access specific patient images and records.

Audit Logs & Accountability

All activity on RadiFind is logged and monitored to ensure complete traceability and accountability for every action taken on the platform.

Search & View

Log in with Single Sign-On (SSO) and secure 2FA. Quickly search for patients and view their radiology images directly within the RadiFind system.

Send & Receive Images

Easily transfer DICOM images between clinics within and outside the RadiFind network. External transfers are handled securely, with encrypted transmission and temporary access links.

Instant Access to All Patient Images

Images are instantly accessible via a DICOM viewer within the platform. All of the patient's available imaging across institutions is displayed within the viewer.

Access Control Dashboard

Manage permissions directly from the admin dashboard. Assign roles and control who can view, request, or share images within the system.

Audit Logs for Accountability

Track every action taken on the platform with detailed audit logs, offering complete transparency over data access and usage.

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) Setup

Secure logins with 2FA for added protection, ensuring only verified users access sensitive information.
